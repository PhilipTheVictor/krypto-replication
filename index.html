ABOUT KRYPTOBONDS

Usually regular bonds offer a 1% to 5% return and they are locked for a fixed number of years. KryptoBonds on the other hand breaks away from this outdated thinking: the customer is not required to hold their K-Bonds for any period of time, growth on their initial investment is expected and they offer the opportunity to win regular significant prizes.

Every KBS bought in KryptoBonds will provide returns either by coin revaluation, monthly dividends or with our fully transparent draws.

The owners of the K-Bonds will hold them due to the expectation of winning the draws causing demand to increase.

The only condition to participate in the draws is to buy or hold K-Bonds.

The prize will be collected from a small fee on each transaction.

KryptoBonds is intended for saving and investment with the added benefit of regular raffles.

In 3 years we intend to be the standard in saving and investment Cryptocurrency reporting sound returns and many satisfied winners!
